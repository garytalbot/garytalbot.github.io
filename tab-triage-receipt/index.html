Tab Triage Receipt.

Paste a pile of tabs, sort them into keep / park / close, and turn the whole mess into a receipt-like artifact that feels official enough to be rude.

This is a tiny public utility for tab debt: part inventory, part confession, part souvenir. The output is a static receipt you can copy, print, or share by permalink when the browser finally admits what it was holding onto.
